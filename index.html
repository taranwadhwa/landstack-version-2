ABOUT LANDSTACK

Why Land Stack for Citizens / Residents

Land Stack Chandigarh is a pilot project to create a unified, transparent, and digital “single source of truth” for all land and property records.

Transparency & Ease,Reduced Disputes & Litigation,Improved Urban Planning & Governance.

Our mission is to modernize land governance in Chandigarh by creating a single source of truth for every land parcel—enhancing transparency, reducing disputes and empowering citizens with real-time, verified property information.

Land Stack

Land Stack integrates geospatial intelligence, digital land records, and modern mapping tools to deliver accurate, efficient, and transparent land-related services for citizens and authorities.

Digital Property Insights

Access reliable property details, updated spatial layers, and verified records that improve transparency and support data-driven decisions.

Transparent Governance System

Enabling faster services, reduced disputes, and seamless coordination between departments through a modern, citizen-centric digital framework.

Smart Land Management

A unified platform that brings accurate land data, modern mapping tools, and streamlined workflows together for efficient land administration.

Next-Generation Land Governance

A modern framework designed to enhance land administration through digital surveys, unified records, and reliable spatial insights for improved planning and service delivery.

Revolutionizing Urban Land Management Through Smart Technology

Land Stack brings together advanced geospatial tools, real-time land data, and digital workflows to simplify surveys, enhance transparency, and support informed decision-making for authorities and citizens.

  • Integrates advanced geospatial tools and real-time land data
  • Simplifies survey operations and ensures accuracy
  • Enhances transparency and minimizes land-related disputes
  • Supports informed decision-making for both citizens and authorities
